My name is Bob Belcher. I am a second-generation restaurateur and the owner of Bob's Burgers. My passion for burgers began while working for my father's restaurant during my childhood. I loved making food that people enjoyed and experiment new recipes and flavours, however, my father would only want me to do things his way and that restricted my creative freedom a lot. That's when I knew I had to open up my own restaurant if I wanted to follow my dream. My favourite part of the day is thinking of new recipes for our "Burger of the day" special. I highly recommend trying it when you stop by our restaurant! I also enjoy spending time with my family and my best friend Teddy.
